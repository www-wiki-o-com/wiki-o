  How To Compose the Statement

Statements must be composed without implication. For example the statement "Anyone with a brain can see that Trump is a genius" implies that the statement is true. It should instead be composed as "Trump is a genius".

The false statement must be the logical complement and must not make any additional implications. For example the statement "Trump is stupid" is not the compliment of "Trump is a genius", "Trump is not a genius" is the complement.

  How To Create Links

A link is created with round brackets, optionally preceeded by a set of square brackets to provide a discription of the link.

For example: [Wiki-O](http://www.wiki-o.com) will render as Wiki-O and (http://www.wiki-o.com) will render as http://www.wiki-o.com.

  How To Create a Bibliography

To create a bibliography, use footnotes [^1] inline with your text followed by [^1]: The footnote text.

For example:

In November 2005, Steven Avery1 was arrested for the murder of Wisconsin photographer Teresa Halbach. In 2007 he was convicted and sentenced to life imprisonment without possibility of parole. The conviction was upheld by higher courts2.

  1. Steven Avery's Wikipedia page.

  2. Making a Murderer.
